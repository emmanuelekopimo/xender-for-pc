Instructions

 

    
                                   

Connecting Laptop

  • Create a Wi-Fi Hotspot on your phone
  • Using the password of the Wi-Fi Network
  • Connect your PC to the Wi-Fi Network

 

Activating on Phone

  • Open the Xender app on your phone
  • Tap the options button 
  • Tap the Connect PC

 

Xender for PC

  • Tap the Connect Phone button
  • Wait for a few seconds for connection
  • To Log out Use the exit button
