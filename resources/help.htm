Connecting PC to Phone's Wi-Fi Network

Slide down to show control center

Long press the hotspot button

To open the Wi-Fi Hotspot settings

 In the hotspot Settings.

Tap name and password to edit.

Set a password you can easily remember.
Turn on on the hotspot switch

Ensure the switch does not go off immediately.

 On your status bar you should see the hotspot icon.

Icon may vary across devices.
Tap the network icon on your taskbar to show available networks.

If your Wi-Fi is turned off. Switch it on.

 Tap 'Connect'. Using the password you set on your mobile phone. Tap next.

You can check the 'Connect automatically' to connect without password next time.

Activating 'Connect PC' mode on your phone

Open the Xender app on your phone.

Tap the options button in the app.

A drop down menu will appear.

 Tap the 'Connect PC' option. This page will appear

You are set to go


Connecting the Xender for PC

  • Open the Xender for PC.
  • Tap 'Link Phone'. Wait for some seconds for linking. About 5 to 10 Seconds
  • Click the accept buton on your mobile phone. It takes you to the home page
  • From here you can navigate the phone storage, download and upload files to your phone.


Taking too long

Solutions

  • Close the Xender app on your phone then reopen. Try linking again

  • Switch off your phones Wi-Fi Hotspot then wait for about 10 Seconds and switch it on again.

  • Close the Xender for PC. Wait for about 5 Seconds. Open the application and try linking again.
  • Reboot your PC and try linking again, reboot your phone and try linking again.

If all solutions are not working then send an Email bixoftware@gmail.com


Error: Device not available

Wi-Fi Device or Drivers not available

Solution: Try reconnecting your Wi-Fi adapter or reinstalling Wi-Fi drivers.

You may require technical assitance.


Error: Device not connected

Wi-Fi not connected

Solution: Try connecting your computer to the Wi-Fi Network you created on your phone


Error: Server not found

Xender app not open or is not in connect PC screen

Solution: Open the Xender app on your phone and go to the connect PC Screen
